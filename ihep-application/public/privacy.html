Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

The Integrated Health Empowerment Program (IHEP) ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Newsletter Signup: Email address for IHEP Progress Brief subscription
  • Inquiry Forms: Information submitted through partnership or contact forms
  • Health Information: For research participants, comprehensive clinical, behavioral, psychosocial, and environmental data collected with explicit consent

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Website Analytics: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site performance
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information
  • To send newsletter communications (with your consent)
  • To process partnership and funding applications
  • To improve our website and services
  • To analyze user behavior and preferences
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • For research and outcome measurement purposes (health information)
  • To conduct clinical studies and generate publications

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent for processing
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to perform a contract with you
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is required by applicable law
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests
  • Health and Research: Processing health data for medical research purposes with IRB approval

5. Data Protection and Security

IHEP implements comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest for all stored data
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • HIPAA-compliant infrastructure on Google Cloud Platform
  • Zero-trust security architecture per NIST SP 800-53r5 standards
  • Multi-factor authentication for all user accounts
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Immutable audit trails for all data access
  • Automated compliance monitoring with continuous scanning

Note: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact Information: Retained as long as you remain on our mailing list or for 3 years after last contact
  • Website Analytics: Retained for 12 months unless longer retention is required for legal compliance
  • Health Research Data: Retained per research protocol timelines and regulatory requirements (typically 7 years minimum)
  • De-identified Data: Retained indefinitely for research and AI model training purposes

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors who assist us in operating our website and services (under data processing agreements)
  • Research Partners: With academic institutions and healthcare systems for approved research (with proper authorization)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • De-identified Data: De-identified data may be shared for research, AI model development, and publication purposes
  • Federated Learning: Health data may participate in federated learning networks where your data never leaves your local site

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to your personal information and receive a copy in a portable format.

8.2 Correction and Updates

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

8.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements and active research protocols.

8.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails or contacting us directly.

8.5 Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our website currently does not respond to DNT signals, but you can disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. International Data Transfers

IHEP operates primarily within the United States. If we collect data from individuals outside the US, we implement appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy determinations where applicable.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to our website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of linked websites.

12. HIPAA Compliance

For research participants and individuals whose health data is processed through IHEP, we comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We have:

  • Designated HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
  • Implemented required administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
  • Executed Business Associate Agreements with all covered entities and subcontractors
  • Established breach notification procedures and incident response protocols

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected
  • Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to equal service and pricing (non-discrimination)

14. EU Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Rights related to automated decision-making

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ihephealth.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@ihephealth.com
  • Mail: Integrated Health Empowerment Program, Newark, New Jersey, USA

16. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
